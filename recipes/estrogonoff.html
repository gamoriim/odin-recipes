Brazilian Stroganoff

Description

Brazilian Beef Stroganoff is a deliciously creamy and comforting dish made with tender beef slices cooked in a rich sauce of cream, onions, and mushrooms. This variation often includes a touch of ketchup and mustard for a slightly tangy and flavorful twist, giving it a distinct Brazilian flair. The dish is traditionally served with white rice and crispy potato sticks (batata palha) on top, adding texture and crunch. It's perfect for a family meal or a special gathering.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • 1 cup white rice (for serving)
  • Potato sticks (batata palha) for garnish

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the white rice according to your preferred method. Set aside for serving.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned. Remove the beef from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onions become soft and translucent.
  5. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook until they are tender and browned.
  6. Add the ketchup, mustard, and beef broth to the pan. Stir well and let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Reduce the heat and add the heavy cream to the pan. Stir until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  8. Return the cooked beef to the pan and stir to combine. Let it cook for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
